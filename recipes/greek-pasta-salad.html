greek-p-salad

This is a great make-ahead pasta salad loaded with the flavors of Greece!

INGREDIENTS

  • half cup olive oil
  • half cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 and half teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 and half teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 and half teaspoons dried oregano
  • 3/4 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 3/4 teaspoons white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups cooked elbow macaroni
  • 3 cups fresh sliced mushrooms
  • 15 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup sliced red bell peppers
  • ¾ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • ½ cup chopped green onions
  • 1 (4 ounce) can whole black olives
  • ¾ cup sliced pepperoni sausage, cut into strips (Optional)

STEPS

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, garlic powder, basil, oregano, black pepper, and sugar together in a large bowl.

  3. Add cooked pasta, mushrooms, tomatoes, red peppers, feta cheese, green onions, olives, and pepperoni. Toss until evenly coated.

  4. Cover, and chill 2 hours or overnight.

  5. Serve and enjoy!
